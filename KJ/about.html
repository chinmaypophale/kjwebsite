How it Works

  1. On signing up you will be taken through a Financial Fitness Experience program (Online or offline)
  2. During this session we will discuss on real psychology of money and success.
  3. Once you understand the real psychology of wealth, we will complete your brief financial planning timeline. (online or offline)
  4. We will calculate all your requirements considering inflation.
  5. For retirement front; we will calculate your wealth number with real rate of returns.
  6. As per requirement; Invest in best mutual funds chosen from our analysis
  7. Your own login id and password will be given for online tracking of all your investment.

In Short; we will guide and train you to be driving seat of your own investments.
